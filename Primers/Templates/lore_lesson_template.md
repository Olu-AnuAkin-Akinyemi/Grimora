## 6. GUIDED PRACTICE (WITH HINTS)

### 6.1 Practice Set (4–6 items)

These are more **thinking / writing / sorting** tasks than calculations, e.g.:

- Sort statements:
  - “Which of these is clear grammar? Which is confusing?”  
- Identify logic patterns:
  - “Which explanation actually gives a reason? Which is just opinion?”  
- Little “fix it” tasks:
  - “Rewrite this sentence to be more precise or more fair.”

Include **hints**:

- “Look for words that could mean more than one thing.”  
- “Ask: does this really follow from the previous idea?”

## 7. INDEPENDENT PRACTICE

### 7.1 Practice Set (6–10 items)

Mix of:

- Short written responses:
  - “Explain Ma’at in your own words to a younger cousin.”  
- Compare / contrast:
  - “How is a fair test in science like a balanced scale?”  
- Mini-analysis:
  - “Find a short explanation (from class, internet, or your life) and mark:
     - its grammar (key words),  
     - its logic (does it follow?),  
     - its rhetoric (is it clear or confusing?).”

These can be done:

- In a physical journal,  
- Or partially in-app via short text boxes.
